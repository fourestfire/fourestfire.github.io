music: 14-song solo album

context

In February 2016, I participated in an annual online songwriting challenge called February Album Writing Month, and successfully wrote and recorded 14 songs before the end of the month. I continued to work on the songs and launched the album in March.

I'm heavily influenced by bands such as the xx, coldplay, and death cab for cutie, and the music I produced could be classified as alt or indie rock.

instruments and equipment

vocals, taylor 314ce acoustic-electric, MIM fender stratocaster, yamaha p-115, logic pro
